# Understanding Fractions

---

## Lesson Goals
- Define numerator and denominator.
- Add and subtract simple fractions.

## Examples
1. **1/2 + 1/2 = 1**
2. **3/4 - 1/4 = 1/2**

## Activity
Students will work in pairs to solve fraction problems on the board.
